Lasanga

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish consisting of layered noodles and filling. When baked it will feed a whole crowd and keep them satisfied for a long while.

Ingredients

  1. 1 package of lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound of lean ground beef
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  5. 1 clove of garlic
  6. half a pound of shredded mozzarella chees
  7. half a pound of shredded cheddar cheese
  8. 1 pint of ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. boil pasta
  2. preheat oven to 350F
  3. in a bowl, combine mozza, cheddar and ricotta; stir well
  4. in a 9x13 pan, alternate layers of filling and pasta unit pan is full
  5. bake at 350F for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbling and melted
